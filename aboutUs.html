Meet the Team

Polly Nectarson

Polly Nectarson is the sweet soul of our beekeeping enterprise. Raised amidst the fragrant blossoms of a family orchard, Polly's love for nature bloomed alongside the apple trees and buzzing bees. Her journey into beekeeping began with a childhood fascination that blossomed into a lifelong passion. Armed with a degree in Botany and a flair for culinary creativity, Polly expertly crafts artisanal honey products that capture the essence of her beloved bees' labor. With each jar of honey sold, Polly not only spreads joy but also raises awareness about the vital role bees play in pollination and ecosystem health.


Hivin' Havenbrook

Standing as the sturdy backbone of our conservational efforts, Hivin' Havenbrook embodies a harmonious blend of practical expertise and unwavering entrepreneurial spirit, infusing every aspect of our hive with resilience and innovation. From a young age, Hivin' found himself drawn to the delicate dance of nature, spending his formative years exploring the wooded trails and meadows surrounding his family home. It was amidst these serene landscapes that his profound respect for the environment took root, guiding him towards a path that seamlessly intertwines business acumen with ecological stewardship. Armed with a degree in Business Management and a heart brimming with passion for conservation, Hivin' embarked on a mission to redefine the boundaries of sustainable beekeeping practices.


Bea Apiarista

Bea Apiarista reigns as the queen bee of Hive Haven, her expertise and passion for conservation guiding our every endeavor. Growing up in the lush countryside surrounded by fields of vibrant wildflowers, Bea developed an early admiration for the intricate dance of pollinators. Pursuing her passion, she earned a degree in Entomology with a focus on bee behavior and ecology. With a gentle touch and a deep understanding of hive dynamics, Bea tends to our colonies with the utmost care, ensuring their health and productivity. Her dedication to sustainable beekeeping practices serves as a beacon of inspiration, empowering others to join the fight for bee conservation.


Buzz McHoneycomb

With a keen sense of humor and a passion for bees, Buzz McHoneycomb is the buzzing brain behind our operations. Growing up on a quaint farm nestled amidst rolling fields of wildflowers, Buzz developed a deep appreciation for the intricate workings of nature, particularly the vital role played by bees. After earning a degree in Environmental Science with a specialization in Apiculture, Buzz embarked on a mission to spread awareness about the critical importance of bee conservation. With every hive tended to with care and every jar of honey harvested sustainably, Buzz is committed to preserving the delicate balance of our ecosystems, one bee at a time.
