Adopt a Hive!





Please select your donor level:



Adopt a Bee!

$5.00

Adopting a bee, is the perfect place to start a donation. It plays a crucial role in supporting our bee conservation efforts. Bees are vital pollinators responsible for the reproduction of many plant species, including those that produce fruits, vegetables, and nuts. By adopting a bee, individuals contribute to the preservation of bee populations, and will recieve a link to follow their bee as they buzz along pollinating.

Adopt a Hive!

$20.00

Adopting a hive represents a significant step forward in supporting our bee conservation initiatives. By embracing a hive, donors play a crucial role in maintaining the health and well-being of these honey makers. In return for their support, donors receive updates and insights into the bustling life of their adopted hive, witnessing firsthand the remarkable efforts these bees undertake in pollinating our ecosystems.

Adopt a Colony!

$50.00

For those seeking to make a substantial impact on bee conservation, adopting a colony offers a powerful opportunity. Colonies consist of thousands of individual bees working together in complex societies. By adopting a colony, donors provide vital funding for large-scale conservation projects aimed at protecting entire bee populations. Donors also gain access to updates about the colony, and broader conservation efforts they're contributing to.
