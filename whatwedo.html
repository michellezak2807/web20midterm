What We Do

Donate to Bee Sanctuaries

When you purchase any honey from us, 100% of your proceeds go to the bees and saving the bees. This could be saving hives from areas they cannot live in, towards research on how to save our wonderful pollinators, and more! This money genuinely makes a difference to our communities, and we thank you for your contributions.

Sustainable Bee Care

Our work with the bees is done in sustainable practices that are both good for the bees we care for, along with the environment, allowing for more generations of bees to flourish. With this, we attempt to work on habitat restoration, so our bees have safe, green, places to live.

Community Engagement

Our team is dedicated towards making the Earth a safer place for the bees, one hive at a time. We are happy to answer any questions from people, which can be reached through our contact us page (where you can also sign up for our email-list!), and happy to continue educating the greater population on the wonder that is our bees.
